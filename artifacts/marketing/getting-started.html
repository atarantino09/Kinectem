Getting Started

Six steps to set up your organization

Every Kinectem org follows the same short setup checklist. Owners and admins see this same list inside the app — here's a preview of what to expect, plus a quick reference of who can do what.

  1. 1

    Upload your org logo

    Give your organization a recognizable face on profiles, team pages, and posts. Square logos look best — at least 256×256.

    Screenshot — logo upload
  2. 2

    Create your first team

    Teams are the unit of activity in Kinectem — they hold rosters, recaps, and highlights. Most orgs start with one team and grow from there.

    Screenshot — create team
  3. 3

    Invite coaches & staff

    Send email invites to coaches, team managers, and front-office staff so they can help run the org alongside you.

    Screenshot — invite staff
  4. 4

    Add a co-admin

    Promote at least one other member to admin so you aren't a single point of failure. Admins can manage teams, members, and posts on behalf of the org.

    Screenshot — promote admin
  5. 5

    Add players to a roster

    From any team page, invite players or add them directly to the roster. Players see team-only content and get tagged in recaps and highlights.

    Screenshot — roster invite
  6. 6

    Invite parents & guardians

    When you invite a player, include the parent's email so they can follow the team and (for players under 13) complete the COPPA consent flow on the child's behalf.

    Screenshot — guardian invite

Roles & permissions

A quick reference for who can do what across the platform. Plain-language summaries, grouped by scope.

Organization roles

  • Owner — Full control. Only the owner can transfer ownership or delete the org. Can do everything an admin can.
  • Admin — Manages org details, teams (create / edit / archive), and members (invite, remove, promote other admins). Can author and approve recaps/posts for any team in the org.
  • Member — Belongs to the org but can't change settings, manage teams, or invite people.

Team roles (roster)

  • Coach — Team manager. Can edit the team, manage its roster (invite / remove players and coaches), and author recaps/posts for that team.
  • Player — On the roster. Sees team-only content and is tagged in team activity. No management rights.
  • Author position — A non-coach roster member can be granted the "author" position to write recaps for that specific team without being made a coach.

Family

  • Parent / Guardian — A user with linked children. Approves follow requests, comments, and DMs for their kids, and manages COPPA consent. Guardian capability is automatic when a child is linked to your account — it's not a separate role you pick.

Platform

  • Platform admin — Internal Kinectem staff. Not something an org grants; mentioned only so people know moderation exists.
